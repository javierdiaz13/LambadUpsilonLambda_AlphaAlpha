Mission Statement

To take a leadership role in meeting the needs of the Latino community through academic achievement, cultural awareness, community service, and promotion of the Latino culture and people.

Who We Are

La Unidad Latina, Lambda Upsilon Lambda Fraternity, Incorporated primarily seeks to take a leadership role in meeting the needs of the Latino community through academic achievement, leadership, promotion of cultural awareness, and community service. Lambda Upsilon Lambda Fraternity, Inc strives to become the premier Latino fraternity in the country. More specifically, we strive to become a nationally recognized Latino fraternal organization comprised of the best and brightest leaders committed to excellence in service and education.

Our Philosophy

The brothers of LUL are strong believers in shaping our own destiny. What separates us from the rest is our ability to inspire greatness in others. We have a remarkable work ethic that allows a small group of men to achieve the impossible, but more importantly create a unique family that we can return to years after. We are small, diverse, and tight knit, but we are not close minded or exclusive.

Our Four Pillars

Academic Excellence

Alpha Alpha strives to serve as a model for academic excellence. In addition to a 3.2 average GPA, LUL's national service initiative, PATHE (Providing Access To Higher Education), focuses on guiding middle and high school students towards college by participating in mentor programs, facilitating tours, granting scholarships, and advocating for the improvement of our educational system. In doing so, LUL hopes to increase the number of opportunities for children and adolescents to surpass their scholastic ambitions.

Cultural Awareness

At Stevens the Alpha Alpha chapter of Lambda Upsilon Lambda supports the efforts to enhance the growth, cultural well-being, and diversity of the student body. You will see brothers heavily engaged with Steven's cultural Recognized Student Organizations (RSO), such as the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE), Latin American Association (LAA), Filipino Association of Stevens Tech (FAST), and the Ethnic Student Counsel (ESC).

Leadership

Alpha Alpha firmly believes in developing leaders that shall provide, improve, and implement the tools for community empowerment. Our brothers are Orientation Leaders, Resident Assisstants, executive board members of cultural and professional organizations, honor society members, and much more across campus. We believe an Hermano of Alpha Alpha should strive to not only be a role model within the fraternity, but also on our campus and beyond.

Community Service

Each year Alpha Alpha collaborates with individuals, organizations, and institutions that will join its efforts to improve the conditions of the Latino community and all underrepresented groups. From volunteering at local community events, to lending our voices to movements we support, to collecting supplies to offer to those less fortunate, Alpha Alpha is continuously involved in helping our surrounding community.
